Slime Rancher 2 — Slimes

Pink Slime

The most common slime on Rainbow Island. Friendly, eats anything, perfect for beginners.

Crystal Slime

Has sharp crystals growing on its body. Dangerous but beautiful — handle with care!

Mosaic Slime

Shines like a disco ball. Shoots sparkling tiles that can hurt you — stunning to watch.

Starfall Slime

Only appears at night under the stars. Glows in the dark and feels magical.

Flutter Slime

Has butterfly wings and flies around. One of the rarest and most beautiful slimes.

Ringtail Slime

Looks like a raccoon. Turns to stone in daylight — sneaky and mysterious!
